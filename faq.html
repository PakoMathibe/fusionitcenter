Got Questions?

What types of devices do you repair at Fusion I.T. Center?

Fusion I.T. Center specializes in repairing a wide range of devices, including laptops, smartphones (such as iPhones, Samsung, and Huawei), tablets, and gaming gear. Our skilled technicians are equipped to handle various hardware and software issues.

Do you provide a warranty for your repair services?

Yes, we stand behind the quality of our repair services. Most of our repairs come with a warranty to ensure your satisfaction and peace of mind. Please check with our team for specific warranty details related to your repair.

Can I sell my old phone to Fusion I.T. Center?

Absolutely! Fusion I.T. Center offers a hassle-free process for selling your old smartphones. We buy phones in any condition, providing competitive prices. Visit our store with your device for a quick evaluation and walk out with cash in hand.

What brands of laptops and smartphones do you sell in your store?

Fusion I.T. Center proudly offers a curated selection of laptops and smartphones from reputable brands. Our inventory includes devices from popular brands like Apple, Samsung, Huawei, and more.

Can I purchase accessories and parts for my devices at Fusion I.T. Center?

Absolutely! Fusion I.T. Center is your one-stop-shop for accessories and genuine parts. Whether you need a laptop charger, phone case, or specific hardware components, our store has a wide range of products to enhance and repair your devices.

Do you provide software development services?

Yes, Fusion I.T. Center offers professional software development services. Whether you need custom software solutions, website design, or mobile app development, our expert team can tailor solutions to meet your specific requirements.

Please contact us if you have any questions about our services or need assistance.
