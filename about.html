Our History
Over nearly two decades at the same location, our company has undergone a remarkable transformation.
Transcending

Starting as Fusion Internet, we've evolved into Fusion I.T. Center, transcending the traditional internet cafe model to become a cutting edge Information Technology Center.

Fierce Competition

In the face of fierce competition in the mid-2000s to mid-2010s internet cafe industry, we not only survived but emerged as the sole survivor.

Great Management

The credit goes to our management's foresight, which led us to diversify our business.

Venturing

We expanded to include administrative services and ventured into the realm of information technology, specializing in the expertise of laptop repair.
